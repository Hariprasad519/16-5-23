Pawan Kalyan Biography

He is an an Indian actor, screenwriter, stunt coordinator, philanthropist and politician.
He started Jana Sena political party on 14th March 2014 for the welfare of people
The main goal of Jana Sena is to question any type of corruption in government functioning and organizations and to protect basic rights of people.
